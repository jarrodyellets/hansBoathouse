--About--

Brussels, Belgium

Hans' Boathouse was founded in 1952 by Hans van Persie. Originally started as a place to share his daily catch with local residents, Hans' Boathouse quickly grew into the seafood destination it is today. Hans believed in quality over quantity, which is why the restaurant is famously known for regularly selling out of food. Located in the heart of Brussels, Hans' Boathouse has evolved from local restaurant, to city landmark. Currently run by his son and daughter, as well as the restaurant cat Nabo, Hans' Boathouse continues to operate with the same quality and tradition as it has for over 60 years.
